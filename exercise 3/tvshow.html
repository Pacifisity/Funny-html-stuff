Bluey

3 Seasons

Available on Disney+

Bluey is an autralian slice of life and parenting show. It features a mother, father and 2 daughters. Throughout each episode it attempts to embody a certain moral teaching whether directly or indirectly. The show is under full creative control of one australian guy and is supposed to be symbolic for his own journey raising two daughters.

Voice Actors

  • David McCormack as Bandit Heeler
  • Melanie Zanetti as Chilli Heeler
  • Dan Brumm as Uncle Stripe Heeler
  • Myf Warhurst as Aunt Trixie Heeler
  • Patrick Brammall as Uncle Radley Heeler
  • Claudia O’Doherty as Frisky
  • Chris Brumm as Chris “Nana” Heeler
  • The kids aren’t credited, their identities are kept secret to shield them from the public eye
